Introduction

Deja Brew

Whether you're going out with friends to grab a beer, or you're having a rough day, it's important to understand the different types of beer to know which beer fits the occasion best. Basic knowledge about types of beer is incredibly important, but it can be overwhelming. This article is designed to help guide you through the beer world, and leave you with a better understanding of which types of beer you will most likely enjoy! We'll break down each beer's contents, how it is made, and how it tastes!
